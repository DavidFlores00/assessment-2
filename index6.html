El Salvador The Hidden Gem

Its Location

El Salvador is a small beautiful country in central america. It is known for its diverse beaches and its many volcanoes. The country has one of the best fresh bodies of water in the world. Over the decades, many people overlooked El Salvador as a tourist destination due to safety concerns. That all changed in 2019 when the new president Nayib Bukele put a stop to all that.

El Tunco Beach

Large rocks and smooth, black sand combine to form the beach along El Tunco. The enormous, angular feature protruding from the sea is the source of even its name. Swimming at the beach can be a little choppy, especially when there are rocks to navigate during high tide. Nevertheless, it's among the top surfing spots in the nation.

Cuisine

El Salvador's traditional cuisine consists of panes con pavo, which are turkey sandwiches served with a special sauce, tamales made of corn dough filled with meat, potatoes, and vegetables, yuca frita, which is fried cassava root, and pupusas, which are thick corn tortillas stuffed with cheese, beans, or meat. Because El Salvador is located near the coast, seafood dishes like ceviche and mariscada are especially well-liked. Mangoes, papayas, pineapples, and other tropical fruits are also used in Salvadoran cuisine.

Bitcoin

Since 2021, El Salvador has ranked as the top bitcoin tourism destination worldwide. As the first nation to accept bitcoin as legal cash, El Salvador has grown to be a popular travel destination for bitcoin enthusiasts. One of El Salvador's biggest draws, besides the natural beauty and friendliness of the people, is the prospect of living in a nation supportive of the economic philosophy of bitcoiners.
